Cancellation & Refund Policy

Transparent policies for all our services

Last Updated: 1st January 2024  |  Effective Date: 1st January 2024

At AutoReview, we believe in complete transparency. This policy outlines the cancellation and refund terms for all services offered on our platform. Please read this carefully before making any purchase or subscription.

1. Free Services

The majority of AutoReview's services are completely free, including:

  • Reading and browsing all car reviews
  • Submitting your own car review
  • Using the car comparison tool
  • Accessing all editorial content and buying guides

No payment is required for these services, and therefore no cancellation or refund applies.

2. Premium Membership (AutoReview Pro)

2.1 What's Included

AutoReview Pro is our optional paid subscription that offers:

  • Ad-free browsing experience
  • Access to exclusive in-depth long-term ownership reports
  • Priority review submission and faster moderation
  • Monthly car buying newsletter with expert recommendations
  • Early access to new car launch reviews

2.2 Subscription Plans & Pricing

  • Monthly Plan: ₹99/month — billed monthly, cancel anytime
  • Annual Plan: ₹799/year — billed annually (save 33%)

2.3 Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your AutoReview Pro subscription at any time by:

  • Logging into your account and visiting Settings → Subscription → Cancel
  • Emailing us at billing@autoreview.in with your registered email and subject "Cancel Subscription"

Upon cancellation:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period
  • You will not be charged for the next billing cycle
  • Access to Pro features will end on the last day of your paid period
  • No partial refunds are issued for unused days in the current billing period

2.4 Refund Policy for Premium Membership

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers only. If you are not satisfied with AutoReview Pro within 7 days of your first subscription, you may request a full refund by contacting us at billing@autoreview.in. After 7 days, no refunds will be issued for the current billing period.

Refunds are not applicable in the following cases:

  • Subscription renewed after the initial 7-day trial period
  • Account suspended due to violation of our Terms & Conditions
  • Partial month or partial year usage after the 7-day window
  • Promotional or discounted subscriptions (unless stated otherwise)

3. Advertising & Sponsored Content

3.1 Advertising Packages

AutoReview offers advertising packages for automotive businesses, dealerships, and brands. These are custom-priced based on placement, duration, and reach.

3.2 Cancellation of Advertising

  • Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 7 business days before the campaign start date for a full refund
  • Cancellations made within 7 days of the campaign start date will incur a 25% cancellation fee
  • No refunds are issued for campaigns that have already started or been published
  • If AutoReview cancels a campaign due to our error, a full refund will be issued within 7 business days

4. Review Removal Requests

If you have submitted a review and wish to have it removed:

  • Email us at reviews@autoreview.in with your name, car model, and reason for removal
  • Review removal is free of charge and will be processed within 48 hours
  • Removal of a review does not entitle you to any refund of any paid services

5. Refund Process

If a refund is approved, it will be processed as follows:

  • Refunds are credited to the original payment method used at the time of purchase
  • Processing time: 5–7 business days for credit/debit cards
  • Processing time: 2–3 business days for UPI and net banking
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is initiated

6. Disputes

If you believe you have been incorrectly charged or have a billing dispute, please contact us within 30 days of the charge at billing@autoreview.in. We will investigate and respond within 5 business days. Disputes raised after 30 days may not be eligible for resolution.

7. Changes to This Policy

AutoReview reserves the right to modify this Cancellation & Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our paid services after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact Us

For any cancellation or refund related queries, please reach out to us:

  • Email: billing@autoreview.in
  • Phone: +91 98765 43210 (Mon–Fri, 10am–6pm IST)
  • Address: AutoReview HQ, Sector 18, Gurugram, Haryana 122015, India
