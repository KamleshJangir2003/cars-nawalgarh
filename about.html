About AutoReview

India's most trusted car review platform since 2018

Our Mission

At AutoReview, we believe every Indian deserves to make an informed car buying decision. Buying a car is one of the biggest financial decisions in life — and we're here to make sure you never regret it. We provide honest, unbiased, and in-depth reviews backed by real-world testing and genuine owner experiences.

What We Stand For

Our core values that drive everything we do

🎯

100% Unbiased

We never accept money from car manufacturers for positive reviews. Our opinions are always independent and honest.

🔬

Thorough Testing

Every car we review is driven for at least 1000 km across different road conditions — city, highway, and off-road.

🇮🇳

Made for India

Our reviews focus on Indian road conditions, fuel prices, service networks, and resale value — not just global specs.

🤝

Community Driven

We amplify real owner voices. Our platform lets actual car owners share their experiences to help future buyers.
